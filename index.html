Privacy Policy

WellPad: Alberta LSD Locator

Effective Date: January 7, 2026
Last Updated: January 7, 2026

The short version: We don't collect your personal data. Everything stays on your device.

Overview

WellPad: Alberta LSD Locator ("WellPad", "the App") is designed with privacy as a core principle. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information.

Information We Do NOT Collect

WellPad does not collect, store, or transmit:

  • Your name, email, or contact information
  • Your location or GPS coordinates
  • Your search history or lookup data
  • Your favorites, notes, or corrections
  • Any personally identifiable information (PII)
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data

Information Stored Locally on Your Device

The following data is stored only on your device and is never transmitted:

Data TypePurposeYour Control
FavoritesQuick access to frequently used LSDsDelete anytime
HistoryRecent lookup convenience (max 50)Clear in Settings
NotesYour personal notes for each LSDDelete anytime
CorrectionsGPS coordinate adjustmentsDelete anytime
SettingsTheme and format preferencesChange in Settings

You own this data. You can export it as JSON, delete it completely, or uninstall the app to remove all data.

Location Access

WellPad requests location permission only for the GPS correction feature:

  • Your device's GPS provides coordinates when you request it
  • Coordinates are stored locally on your device only
  • No location data is ever transmitted to any server
  • You can deny location permission and still use all other features

Crash Reporting

WellPad uses Firebase Crashlytics to help fix bugs. When the app crashes:

What IS collected: Crash stack trace, device model, Android version, app version

What is NOT collected: Your location, search queries, favorites, history, notes, or any identifying information

Crash reports are anonymous and contain no personally identifiable information.

Third-Party Services

Firebase Crashlytics

Purpose: Crash reporting to improve app stability. Data collected: Anonymous crash data only.

Privacy policy: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

External Map Applications

When you tap "Navigate", WellPad opens your chosen map app with the LSD coordinates. Those apps have their own privacy policies.

Data Security

  • All data is stored in your device's private app storage
  • Data is protected by Android's app sandboxing
  • No data is transmitted over the internet (except anonymous crash reports)
  • Uninstalling the app deletes all stored data

Children's Privacy

WellPad does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app is designed for professional field workers.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data (use Export Data feature)
  • Delete your data (use Clear All Data feature)
  • Deny location permission
  • Uninstall the app at any time
