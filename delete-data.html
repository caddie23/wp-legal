Delete Your Data

App: WellPad: Alberta LSD Locator

Developer: WellPad

How to Delete All Your Data

WellPad stores all data locally on your device. You can delete all your data directly within the app:

  1. Open WellPad on your device
  2. Tap the Settings tab (bottom navigation)
  3. Scroll to the Data section
  4. Tap "Clear All Data"
  5. Confirm when prompted

What Gets Deleted

  • All saved favorites
  • All lookup history
  • All notes
  • All GPS corrections
  • All app preferences
Note: This action cannot be undone. Consider using the "Export Data" option first if you want to keep a backup.

Data Not Stored on Our Servers

WellPad is an offline-first app. Your data is stored only on your device and is never transmitted to our servers. There is no cloud account to delete.

Uninstalling the App

Uninstalling WellPad from your device will also delete all app data.
